iOS app with Cook support

CookApp - Recipe reader for Cooklang

Promo text Make cooking as quick and easy as possible with CookApp!

Description Save your time - Browse recipes by time-to-prepare, create meal plans, and optimize your shopping list. Save your money - We charge once. No subscriptions, no microtransactions. Own your data - Maintain complete control and ownership of your data.

Note: Requires a Mac to create and edit recipes

Features:

  • Read recipe files written in the Cooklang markup language.
  • Import recipes from the community recipe repository.
  • Create meal plans for each week, and then generate a shopping list.
  • Export shopping lists to email.
